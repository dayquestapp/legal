# Day Quest — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 9 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Day Quest mobile application
("Day Quest", "the app", "we", "us") handles information.

Day Quest is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not run
servers, we do not collect personal information, and we do not transmit
your data to anyone.

## Information we do not collect

Day Quest does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

- Your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifier
- Your location
- Your contacts, photos, or device files
- Your usage analytics or behavioural data
- Crash reports or diagnostic information sent to us
- Advertising identifiers
- Any data that could identify you personally

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs
of any kind.

## Information stored on your device

All content you create in Day Quest — including quests, subtasks, journal
entries, timers, themes, statistics, and settings — is stored locally on
your device. This data never leaves your device through Day Quest.

If you have iCloud enabled and the system backs up your device, your Day
Quest data may be included in that backup. This backup is between you and
Apple under Apple's terms; we have no access to it.

## Purchases and subscriptions

Day Quest offers in-app purchases and subscriptions through Apple's App
Store using StoreKit. When you make a purchase:

- The transaction is handled entirely by Apple.
- We never see, receive, or store your payment information.
- Apple provides Day Quest with a receipt confirming whether you have an
  active entitlement, so the app can unlock premium features. This receipt
  contains no personal information.
- Subscription management (cancellation, refunds, billing) is handled by
  Apple in the App Store account settings on your device.

For details on how Apple handles purchase data, see Apple's privacy policy:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Notifications

If you grant notification permission, Day Quest schedules local
notifications on your device to remind you of quests and timers. These
notifications are generated entirely on-device and are not sent through
any server.

## Children

Day Quest is suitable for general audiences and is not directed to
children under 13. Because Day Quest collects no personal information from
anyone, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your rights

Because Day Quest does not collect or store any personal information off
your device, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, export,
or delete on your behalf. You can delete all Day Quest data at any time by
uninstalling the app from your device.

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or
California and have questions about your rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or
the CCPA, you can contact us at the email below.
